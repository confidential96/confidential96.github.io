Broken Twill


Brand Development & Representation

 

Broken Twill is a retail focused design studio, developing brands and e-commerce platforms since 2010.

Between locations in Germany and the UK we supply a full service solution for fashion centric manufacturers, brands and retailers.

Design


Brand, campaign and e-commerce development.

 

Broken Twill is the UK's leading menswear e-commerce specialist. Pioneering a design language that has been adopted industry wide.

Take a look at some of our recent work: 

 
Glen Milner

Glen Milner is a documentary and commercial filmmaker based in London. He has shot and directed films covering a wide range of global topics for broadcasters such as Channel 4, BSKYB, The Guardian, The Telegraph, UNICEF  & others. We created a portfolio site to house his expansive portfolio. Powered by Wordpress CMS.

The Mandon Store

A magento powered e-commerce store for renowned e-trailer The Mandon Store. Built to withstand. 

  

Porters Menswear

E-commerce site for UK based fashion retailer Porters. 

"Broken Twill have been a pleasure to work with. They strove to find solutions to all of our requirements no matter what these were. They designed a site that exceeded our expectations, launching on time and with a great reaction from our customers." 

Russell Athletic Archive

The Russell Manufacturing Co was founded in 1902 in Alexander City, Alabama by Benjamin Russell as a producer of woolen under- garments. Today Russell Athletic is an internationally recognised brand. We worked with them on their heritage line, Russell Athletic Archive.

  

Trainerspotter

E-commerce site for highly acclaimed street wear brand Trainerspotter.com

Slowdownjoe

Slowdownjoe is a forerunner of a new breed of curiosity style online retailers. With a Americana 'General Store' feel we built them a minimal but feature rich online store.

Marrkt

A flash sale site from the founders of Superdenim. Exclusive and limited time sales on niche and high end menswear products.

Produkt Workshop

A collaborative project between Broken Twill and the people behind Heritage Research. An experimental menswear and accessories line launching soon. 

Eastman Leather Motorcycle Club

Early stage concept work for a new project launching soon by leather specialist Gary Eastman.

Interactive Suit Builder

A highly progressive online suit designer custom designed and built for the BMB group. Using over 1000 CGI rendered images the customer can design their suit online and then place an order after providing their measurements.

Winter Crown

Branding and e-commerce development for a new knitwear label, launching later this year.
